Answers to frequently asked questions

Quite a number of people question our ambitions and do not share our goals. Many of them have reasonable questions that we simply must answer.

  • 01 Will you allow a multiparty system in our country?

    In our opinion, the only correct solution in this situation will be to preserve the one-party system in our country. The strongest survives, and we fully agree with this statement. The rest of the parties missed their chance, but they can still join the government as members of our party.

  • 02 What kind of relationships will you maintain with other countries?

    Obviously good if possible. Our country will personify a kind of pacifist in the world of other states. Immediately after coming to power, our party has already concluded some peaceful and economically beneficial agreements with neighboring countries.

  • 03 Will military service be compulsory in our country?

    We decided to leave this branch of the system unchanged, since any country (and ours is no exception) must have some kind of power to protect its citizens
